The Intelligent Dolphin

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their agility and playful behavior. They are carnivores, mostly eating fish and squid. They are social creatures, living in pods and communicating using a complex system of clicks and whistles. This section demonstrates a simple two-column layout using basic CSS float properties.
