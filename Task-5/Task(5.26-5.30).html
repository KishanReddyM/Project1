2. Ordered List: Recipe for Pasta Aglio e Olio

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add 200g of spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta cooks, thinly slice 4-6 cloves of garlic.
  4. In a large pan, heat 1/4 cup of olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the garlic and a pinch of red pepper flakes, cooking until the garlic is golden (do not burn).
  6. Once cooked, drain the pasta, reserving a cup of the pasta water.
  7. Add the drained pasta to the pan with the garlic and oil. Toss to combine.
  8. Add a splash of reserved pasta water to create a light sauce.
  9. Stir in a handful of fresh parsley, season with salt, and serve immediately.

4. Definition List: Web Technologies

CSS

5. Comparison: Frontend vs. Backend

Frontend (Client-Side)

  • Deals with the user interface (UI)
  • What the user sees and interacts with
  • Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Frameworks: React, Vue, Angular
  • Focuses on user experience (UX)

Backend (Server-Side)

  • Deals with the server, database, and logic
  • The "brains" of the application
  • Technologies: Node.js, Python, Java
  • Frameworks: Express, Django, Spring
  • Focuses on data, security, and performance
