float: left | right;

The float property places an element on the left or right side of its container, allowing text and inline elements to wrap around it. The element is removed from the normal flow of the page.

1. Float Image to the Left of Text

This paragraph text wraps around the floated image. The float property is incredibly useful for this kind of layout, which is common in articles and blogs. The image is taken out of the normal document flow, and the rest of the content flows around it. We've added a `clearfix` to the parent container to ensure it properly contains the floated image.
