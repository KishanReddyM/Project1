Task 14 & 15: content-box vs. border-box

Both boxes have `width: 100%`, `padding: 20px`, and a `10px` border. Notice how `border-box` includes padding and border within the width, preventing overflow.

1. `box-sizing: content-box;` (Default)

Total width = 100% + 40px padding + 20px border

2. `box-sizing: border-box;`

Total width = 100%
